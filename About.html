Our Story

For over two decades, Taste of India has been serving authentic Indian cuisine with a modern twist, creating unforgettable dining experiences in the heart of the city.

Discover Our Journey

Our Journey

Founded in 2001 by Chef Rajesh Sharma, Taste of India began as a small eatery with just 10 tables and a dream to bring the authentic flavors of India to our community. Chef Rajesh, who grew up in the vibrant streets of Delhi, wanted to share the culinary traditions passed down through generations of his family.

What started as a humble neighborhood restaurant has grown into an award-winning dining destination, recognized for its innovative approach to classic Indian dishes while staying true to traditional techniques and spices.

Today, under the guidance of Chef Rajesh and his daughter Priya, we continue to evolve while maintaining the warmth and authenticity that has defined us from the beginning. Every dish tells a story, and every meal is an invitation to experience the rich culinary heritage of India.
