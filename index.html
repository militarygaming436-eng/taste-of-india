Our Journey

Founded in 1998 by Master Chef Rajesh Kumar, Taste of India began as a small family restaurant with a dream to share authentic Indian flavors with the world. Today, we're proud to be one of the most celebrated Indian restaurants, known for our traditional recipes and warm hospitality.

Our Philosophy

We believe food is an expression of love. Every dish is prepared with meticulous care, using time-honored techniques passed down through generations. From sourcing the finest spices to handcrafting each meal, we pour our heart into every aspect of your dining experience.
